Frequently Asked Questions Find answers to common questions or contact us for help.
What is AlgoTrade?
Algotrade is a trading bot platform designed to automate the buying and selling of financial assets based on pre-defined algorithms. It allows users to create and deploy trading strategies and selections without needing to manually execute trades, aiming to optimize trading efficiency and profitability.
How much of my profits do i get to keep on Algotrade?
On Algotrade, you get to keep 93% of the profits generated by our bots. The platform takes 7% of your earnings.
What's the minimum deposit amount required to start trading with Algotrade?
The minimum deposit required to access our bots is $150. While a higher deposit is recommended to provide more capital for the bots to work with, the minimum deposit allows you to get started. Please note that your deposit amount will impact your trading results, and you should only invest what you can afford to risk. Remember, the higher you deposit, the more gains you are keen to make. We suggest if you deposit as high as $1000, you set your trading percentage to 6% per trade using any of the bots.
What types of assets do the trading bots trade with?
Our bots trade a wide variety of assets, constantly scanning for optimal trading opportunities across multiple markets, including cryptocurrencies, forex, stocks, commodities and even arbitrage opportunities. The bots dynamically adjust their focus to capitalize on the most profitable trades available.
